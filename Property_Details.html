An architectural masterpiece where the sky meets the water.

Designed by world-renowned architect Elena Rossi, The Azure Pavilion represents the pinnacle of tropical modernism. Located on the pristine shores of Bacalar's Laguna de los Siete Colores, this property harmonizes organic materials with precision engineering.

The residence features a 40-foot cantilevered living area that floats above the water, offering unobstructed 270-degree views of the crystalline lagoon. Every detail, from the hand-carved Oaxacan stone walls to the custom-milled teak cabinetry, has been curated to provide a sanctuary of "quiet luxury."
